# RakuOS Community Code of Conduct

## Our Purpose
The RakuOS community exists to build, improve, and support the operating system. Participation means agreeing to follow this Code of Conduct.

Our focus is technical collaboration, not personal beliefs or political advocacy.

## Core Principles

### Respect First
- Assume good intent.
- Focus on ideas, not individuals.
- Communicate politely and constructively.
- Accept different experience levels and perspectives.

### Stay Technical
- Keep discussions relevant to RakuOS development, support, documentation, or features.
- Avoid off-topic debates, especially political or ideological.

### Political and Social Neutrality
- RakuOS is a technical community.
- Do **not** promote political parties, campaigns, or causes.
- Avoid political or ideological debates of any kind.
- Respect all members; personal beliefs are private and irrelevant here.

### Inclusion & Non-Discrimination
- Everyone is welcome.
- Do **not** discriminate based on race, ethnicity, national origin, religion, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, age, disability, or other personal attributes.
- Provide guidance and support to all contributors.
- Use clear, inclusive, and respectful language.
- Avoid gatekeeping or elitism.

### Constructive Communication
**Encouraged:**
- Clear bug reports and reproducible issues
- Technical discussion with reasoning or data
- Thoughtful feature proposals
- Respectful code review and feedback

**Not acceptable:**
- Harassment, insults, or name-calling
- Mocking, shaming, or belittling others
- Threats or intimidation
- Trolling or intentionally disruptive behavior
- Discrimination or exclusion

### Contribution Expectations
- Follow project guidelines and workflows.
- Keep contributions focused and documented.
- Be open to review and feedback.
- Respect maintainer decisions.
- Focus on technical improvement rather than personal beliefs.

### Reporting Issues
- Report violations to project maintainers or moderators.
- Provide relevant details or links when possible.
- Reports will be handled respectfully and confidentially.

### Enforcement
Maintainers may take action to maintain a respectful, productive environment, including:
- Requesting behavior changes
- Removing content
- Temporary or permanent removal from community spaces
- Restricting contribution access

---

**Be respectful. Be constructive. Keep it technical. Help others succeed.**
